OUR LOCATION

Pen, a Tuitt Bootcamp Trainee with rights to famous programming languages, became the first owner of this gym. He had a perfect location in Fairview near Novaliches. The original space was only one thousand square meters so a lot of negotiating ensued to get another eight hundred square meters of space. For the next clubs, a new partnership was forged between Vince, Pat, Sean, Dennis, Jen, Mark, Sid and all of his classmates, and the Tuitt family. We started with Fairview and Manila branches. From the start until 2005, we were under a Galactic company which got the developmental rights for the brand for several countries including the Philippines.
